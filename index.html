About

Audia plays podcasts through your phone line, without using up your costly data!

Audia will dial you into one of our supported podcasts, allowing you to listen in, without the hastle of downloading each episode.

  • Stream podcasts without needing a data connection, saving you money! No longer do you have to worry about data speeds and data caps when all you want to do is listen to podcasts.
  • Listen to the latest podcasts - Audia will automatically connect you to the latest episode the first time you dial in. No need to worry about synching or downloading the lastest episodes yourself.
  • Audia does the hard part your you. The app keeps track of all the numbers and automatically dials you into the right podcast letting you just focus on listening.
  • Don't have a smartphone? Dial in on any phone- smart, dumb, or landline- and you can still listen to your favorite podcasts

Hear podcasts. Anywhere.

Audia works anywhere you have phone service! Even if saving data is not an issue for you, Audia works great on your commute when you have a spotty data connection.

Browse podcasts through a simple and clean interface with our Android app

We built our app to get you straight to listening. Subscribe to podcasts so you can keep track of your favorite podcasts. Don't worrry about losing your spot because when you hang up Audia will remember where you left off. The next time you listen, Audia will get you right back to the moment where you last left off.
